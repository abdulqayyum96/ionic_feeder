Ionic Baby Feeder

Track your baby's feeding habits.

Download Alpha here

Beautifully simple

Ionic Baby Feeder tracks feedings in style. It emphasizes what's important - time since last feeding, duration of ongoing feeding and volume of bottle feedings.

Swipe from day to day to see past feedings. Set a buzz every 5 minutes to keep track of time without looking.

Syncs all your devices

Use Ionic Baby Feeder on your phone, your tablet, your partner's phone or any Android device. They all sync.

Private and personal

No fees, no ads, no accounts, no email checks, no bloat, no nothing. It's a God damn baby feeding app. Why would we need to know your shoe-size or who your buddy Charlene's hanging out with? Screw that.

Built using Ionic framework.
