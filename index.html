Ionic Baby Feeder

Track your baby's feeding habits.

Download from Google play Web version

Beautifully simple

Ionic Baby Feeder tracks breast and bottle feedings in style. It emphasizes what's important - which breast is in turn, time since last feeding and duration/volume of current feeding.

Syncs all your devices

Use Ionic Baby Feeder on your phone, your tablet, your partner's phone or any Android device. Now even a light version on iPhones and your computer(*). They all sync.

Private and personal

No fees, no ads, no accounts, no email checks, no bloat, no nothing. It's a God damn baby feeding app. Why would we need to know your shoe-size or who your buddy Charlene's hanging out with? Screw that.

(*)Technically. Since it works on Chrome/Safari/Opera you can use the web-version and that will sync too.
