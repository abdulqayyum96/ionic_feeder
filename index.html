Ionic baby feeder

Track your baby's feeding habits.

Download Alpha here

Beautifly simple

Ionic baby feeder tracks feedings in style emphesizing what's important - time since last feeding and duration of ongoing feeding.

Swipe from day to day to see past feedings. Set a buzz to vibrate your phone every 5 minutes to keep track without looking at the phone.

Syncs all your devices

Use Ionic Baby Feeder on your phone, your tablet, your partner's phone or any other Android device. They all sync.

No bloat

No fees, no ads, no accounts, no email checks, no nothing. It's a God damn baby feeding app. Why would we need to know your shoe-size?
